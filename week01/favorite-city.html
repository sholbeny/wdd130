Heidelberg, Germany

Heidelberg Germany was my very first city when I went on my mission. I consider it my "German Home" just because of all the memories I created there.

The city is full of history, from the Heidelberg Castle, to the Old University, to the Church of the Holy Spirit. One of the coolest sights is hidden up in the mountain across from the Heidelberg Castle, an amphitheatre that was used by the Nazi's during World War II
